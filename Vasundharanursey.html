About Us

Cultivating Dreams,
Growing Excellence

Vasundhara Nursery is a trusted plant nursery located in the heart of Panchavati, Nashik. We specialize in offering premium-quality fruit plants, sandalwood plants, and garden varieties for homes, farms, and commercial plantations.

Our commitment to sustainable farming practices and expert horticultural knowledge ensures that every plant we deliver thrives in its new home.
