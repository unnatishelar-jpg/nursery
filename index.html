Our Story

Rooted in Tradition, Growing for Tomorrow

Vasundhara Nursery stands as a beacon of horticultural excellence in the sacred lands of Panchavati, Nashik. For over 15 years, we have nurtured plants with dedication, transforming barren lands into flourishing orchards and gardens.

Our expertise spans from premium grafted fruit trees to the highly coveted sandalwood plantations, each grown with meticulous care and sustainable practices.
